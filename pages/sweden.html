Women in the history of Sweden

Feminism in Sweden is a significant social and political influence within Swedish society. Swedish feminism dates back to the 17th century and was discussed in intellectual circles throughout the 18th century.

Universal women suffrage followed in 1921. Since then, Sweden has remained a forerunner of gender equality driven by a both intellectual and practical feminist movement.
